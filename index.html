How It Works

Manage

Track all jobs in real time 24/7 on one platform with multiple operators. Instantly know job delivery statuses, at their precise locations and timings.

Plan

Save hours by scheduling thousands of jobs in seconds with a single click. Optimize driver routes instantly so fewer trucks go around empty - save on mileage and deliver more quickly.

While fleet visibility is important, we believe in the freedom to choose your own GPS provider. We specialise in planning, and have pre-integrated with a range of partners. Introduce us if your preferred vendor is not on our list!

Dispatch

VersaFleet™ lets operators dispatch runsheets 'live' and change them on the fly. With VersaDrive™, the mobile companion app, every driver will always have the most current job details and can send updates instantly with photos and e-signatures.
